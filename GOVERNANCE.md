## Roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| **Maintainer** | Final say on roadmap and merges; publishes releases; enforces Code of Conduct with community help. |
| **Contributor** | Anyone who opens issues or PRs; follows CONTRIBUTING and Code of Conduct. |
| **Collaborator** *(optional)* | Trusted contributors with merge rights — granted by maintainers when the codebase warrants it. |

---

## Decision-making

1. **Day-to-day:** Maintainers review PRs and respond to issues. Small fixes and docs can merge after maintainer approval.
2. **Product direction:** Large features should align with [**GitBattle_Project_Proposal.md**](GitBattle_Project_Proposal.md) unless maintainers explicitly decide to change scope (document major shifts in CHANGELOG or proposal updates).
3. **Disputes:** Prefer good-faith discussion in issue/PR threads. If blocked, maintainers make a decision and document the rationale briefly.
